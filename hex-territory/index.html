Hex Territory Strategy

Claim empty hexes, surround enemy clusters, and flip them to your color. You place blue stones, the AI places red stones, and the fuller board decides the winner.

Turn: Player

Rules

Click any empty hex on your turn. After each move, any adjacent enemy cluster with no path to an empty hex is captured and changes color.

Multiplayer

Online mode is peer-to-peer. Host shares the offer JSON, guest pastes it and replies with an answer JSON, then both sides can play blue vs red across the network.
