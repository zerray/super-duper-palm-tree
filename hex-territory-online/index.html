Hex Territory Online

An online-upgraded version of Hex Territory. Play solo against the built-in AI or use a WebRTC peer connection so two browsers can battle over the same board together.

Mode: Solo vs AI
Turn: Blue
Blue: 0
Red: 0
Empty: 127

Rules

Click any empty hex on your turn. After each move, any adjacent enemy cluster with no path to an empty hex is captured and changes color.

Match Mode

Solo mode is active. Switch to online mode to host or join a peer-to-peer room.

Online Handshake

Host: click Host Match, send the local signal to your opponent, then paste their answer into remote signal and apply it. Joiner: paste the host offer, click Join Match, then send your generated answer back to the host.

Status

Your turn. Pick any empty hex to start expanding.
