Hex Chain Reaction

Two players alternate on a compact hex grid. Click an empty hex or one you already own, then try to force overflowing chain reactions that convert neighboring cells to your color.

Turn: Blue
Status: Blue to move.
Board: 7 × 7

Match Controls

Local hot-seat only. Chain reactions resolve one wave at a time before the turn changes.

Live State

Blue cells: 0
Red cells: 0
Cascade step: 0

Blue begins. Corner hexes overflow later than center hexes because capacity matches live neighbor count.

Rules

Blue plays first. Red is the second local player.
You may only place a token on an empty or friendly cell.
If token count exceeds neighbor count, the excess bursts outward and converts every touched neighbor.

Blue Wins

One player controls every occupied hex.
