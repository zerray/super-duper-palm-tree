Rules

Blue and red alternate turns in hot-seat mode. You may only place on an empty hex or one you already control.
Every added token can strengthen your frontier or set up a forced conversion sequence on the next burst.
When tokens exceed a cell's neighbor count, it explodes outward, converts adjacent cells, and keeps chaining until the board stabilizes.
