Hex Colony

Place one structure per turn, grow your border, and keep the blight away from the settlement long enough to claim 50 hexes.

Turn 1
Gold 5
Territory 1 / 50
Blight in 3

Build

You can only build on neutral hexes adjacent to your territory. Farms generate 1 gold per turn, towers claim adjacent neutral hexes after 2 turns, and walls stop blight from entering that hex.

Status

Select a structure, then click a highlighted hex next to your territory.

Rules

Costs: farm 2 gold, tower 3 gold, wall 1 gold. Blight spreads from edge blight tiles into unoccupied hexes every 3 turns. You lose if blight reaches the settlement.
