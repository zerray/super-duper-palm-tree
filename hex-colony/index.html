Terrain Rules

Forest grants +2 lumber with Village and +1 growth when paired with Forest.
Farm gains +3 food by Lake and +2 growth by Village.
Village gains +2 lumber by Forest, +2 growth by Farm, and +3 ore trade by Mine.
Mine gains +3 ore trade by Village and +2 springs by Lake.
Lake feeds Farms and cools Mines for bonus value.

Sandbox Rules

Board radius uses cube coordinates for six-way adjacency.
Tiles can be placed on any empty hex that touches your colony.
Synergy connections flash between the new tile and matching neighbors.
Reach the target population score before the placement limit ends.
