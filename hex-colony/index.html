Hex Colony

Grow a tiny kingdom one hex at a time. Farms fund expansion, towers claim nearby land after two turns, and walls are the only reliable answer when blight creeps in from the rim.

Turn 1
Gold 5
Territory 1 / 50
Blight 6

Build

Choose one structure, then click an empty hex adjacent to your territory. Every placement ends the turn.

Status

Place your first tile next to the settlement. Reach 50 controlled hexes before blight reaches home.

Rules

Owned empty territory can still be consumed by blight.
Farms earn 1 gold at the end of every turn.
Towers convert adjacent neutral hexes to player territory after 2 turns.
Walls stop blight from entering that hex.
Blight spreads from the edges every 3 turns.
