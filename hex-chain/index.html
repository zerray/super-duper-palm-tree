The AI checks every empty hex and color, then greedily picks the move with the best immediate chain score. Ties lean toward blocking your best matching chain.

Rules

A chain is any connected group of adjacent hexes with the same color owned by the same side. Scoring is exponential: a chain of length n is worth 2^(n-1) points on the turn it is created or extended.

The game ends when the 7x7 hex grid is full. Highest score wins.
