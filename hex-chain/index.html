Rules

A matching neighbor extends your chain. Each placement scores 2^(chain length - 1), so small links are cheap but a long run can swing the match fast. The board fills, then the higher score wins.

Heuristic AI

The AI tests every empty hex and every color. It first maximizes its own immediate chain score, then breaks ties by limiting your next best move.
