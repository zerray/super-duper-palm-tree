Choose a Color

Pick a swatch to flood your connected territory. You cannot choose your current color or the AI's current color.

Rules

The board starts as a 7x7 hex grid with six saturated colors. Your root is the bottom-left hex; the AI starts at the top-right hex. Flood-fill spreads through adjacent neutral hexes that match the chosen color, and the game ends when all hexes are claimed.

Markers

Diamond markers show the player root and the AI root so you can track each connected region during the flood animations.

Online Match

Host plays the bottom-left empire, guest plays the top-right empire. The host validates picks and sends the full board after each turn.
